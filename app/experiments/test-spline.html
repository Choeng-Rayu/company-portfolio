Frequently Asked Questions


Why should I pick you guys instead of hiring my own in-house developer or designer?
+
Great question! Hiring an in-house, senior-level developer can indeed be an investment, often exceeding $60,000 annually, not to mention additional benefits. That's quite the commitment, particularly if your workload isn't consistent. On the other hand, we offer a flexible, on-demand service model. Our subscription can be customized to your needs; it's adaptable, letting you pause, resume, or adjust according to your project demands. This way, you're paying for the actual work, not idle time. So, you receive high-quality services without the obligation of a full-time salary.
Are there limits to my design requests?
+
Absolutely not. We provide boundless design services catered to your needs. Your dedicated designer or developer will work on your tasks every business day, keeping you updated on progress or switching to a new task once the current one is complete. The timeline may vary based on the complexity of your request, but rest assured, we will update you everyday.
When will I get my hands on my creations?
+
On your marks, get set, develop! Usually, we'll cross the finish line with your request within two days or less. But if you're asking us to build Rome, remember, it wasn't built in a day. The more complex your request, the longer the creative journey.
Can you explain the 'pause' feature?
+
Certainly. We understand there might be periods when you require fewer services. Our 'pause' feature allows you to hold your subscription and only pay for the days you've utilized. For example, if you've used our service for 11 days and then decide to pause, we will hold the remaining 20 days for up to six months until you're ready to resume.
What if the design doesn't set my heart aflutter?
+
Didn't love it at first sight? No sweat. We'll keep honing your design until it has your heart doing backflips.
Is the monthly web hosting fee included in the subscription?
+
While our subscription does not encompass web hosting costs, we're equipped with the expertise to guide you through setting up cost-effective hosting tailored to your project needs. If preferred, we can even manage your hosting. However, please note that this will incur additional charges based on your specific requirements. The Mainichi subscription, while comprehensive, does not cover ancillary costs such as hosting, domain purchase, certifications, or necessary plugins for deployment.
Why should I consider technical support?
+
Technical support covers crucial website maintenance for aspects like software updates, speed optimization, HTML error fixes, file backups, fix broken links, and SEO. Slow and old websites increase bounce rates and reduce search visibility. Our service optimizes speed for a better user experience and improved SEO.
